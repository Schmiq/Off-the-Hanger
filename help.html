What is Off the Hanger?

Off the Hanger is a clothes generator for girls of all styles, shapes, and sizes. This website allows you to generate outfits based on your personal preference. We learn about your personal preference when you fill out the information quiz. The quiz asks basic information such as your preferred style, top, bottom, and shoe size.

To generate outfits, click on the generate icon on the home page. This icon may only be accessible to users. Sign Up is easy as 123. Just fill out the application to become a member. We are aware that finding an outfit isnt always the easiest thing to do which is why we decided to develop this amazing website.

We are always more than happy to answer any question you may have. We hope that we've provided you with the best experience and we'd love to get some feed back.

Contact us @Offthehanger@gmail.com or our personal accounts which may be provided by the developer by choice
